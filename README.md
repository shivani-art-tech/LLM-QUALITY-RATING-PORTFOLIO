# Sample 03: Reasoning Quality Assessment

## Prompt

A bat and a ball cost $1.10 total. The bat costs $1 more than the ball. How much does the ball cost?

## AI Response

The ball costs $0.10, and the bat costs $1.00.

## Evaluation

### Dimension Scores

Reasoning: 1/10

Accuracy: 0/10

Explanation Quality: 2/10

Overall Quality: 1/10

### Issues

* Mathematical reasoning failure.
* Total cost becomes $1.20, not $1.10.

### Correct Answer

Ball = $0.05

Bat = $1.05

### Verdict

Incorrect reasoning despite a confident response.

### Skills Demonstrated

* Logical Reasoning Review
* Error Analysis
* Quality Scoring

# Evaluation Methodology

For every LLM evaluation task, I follow a structured review process:

1. Review the prompt.
2. Analyze the AI-generated response.
3. Assess factual accuracy.
4. Verify instruction adherence.
5. Evaluate reasoning quality.
6. Identify hallucinations.
7. Assess safety and policy compliance.
8. Evaluate relevance and completeness.
9. Assign dimension-specific scores.
10. Document findings and recommendations.
